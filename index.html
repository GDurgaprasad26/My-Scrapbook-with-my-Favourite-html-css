Favourite Games

CHESS

Chess first appeared in India about the 6th century ce as a game called chaturanga.
By the 10th century it had spread from Asia to the Middle East and Europe.
Since at least the 15th century, chess has been known as the “royal game”.


CIRCKET

Cricket is played on either circular or oval shaped grounds.
There is a rectangular area in the middle of the ground, called the pitch.
which is the most important part of the field. On the pitch, all the bowling and batting takes place.


SHUTTLE

A game similar to footbag or hackysack where players aim to keep the shuttlecock from touching the ground.
A weighted shuttlecock usually made of a plain washer attached to feathers, plastic straw rope or plastic candy wrapper.

Favourite Hobbies.

LISTENING MUSIC

The delicate artifact is the oldest known musical instrument on earth — indicating that people have been making music for over 40,000 years.

WALKING IN MORNING

Morning walks, especially on an empty stomach, are said to burn more fat and energise the heart by kickstarting circulation early in the day.
Research supports that exercising in the morning can improve adherence to a routine, people tend to stick to morning schedules more than evening ones.

Bike Riding

Cycling is an enjoyable way to stay healthy.
It can also help you lose weight, lower your cholesterol, and improve your balance.
Cycling is a low impact aerobic exercise that offers a wealth of benefits.

Favourite Food Items.

Biryani

Biryani is a quintessential Indian dish, a symphony of flavors and textures where fragrant basmati rice meets succulent marinated meat (or vegetables),
all layered with aromatic spices and often adorned with fried onions, fresh herbs, and a drizzle of ghee.

Fish Fry

A fish fry is a social event containing battered or breaded fried fish.
It usually also includes french fries, coleslaw, macaroni salad, lemon slices, tartar sauce, hot sauce, malt vinegar and dessert.
Some Native American versions are cooked by coating fish with semolina and egg yolk.
